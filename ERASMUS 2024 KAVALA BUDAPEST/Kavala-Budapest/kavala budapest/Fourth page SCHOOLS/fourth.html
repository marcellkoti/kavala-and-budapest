Bethlen Gábor Technikum
3o Epal Alexandroupoli
Bethlen Gábor Technikum is a secondary school in Budapest XV. district, Újpalota.
An elementary school first started operating in the building in 1982, under the name Árendás közi Áltánosás Iskola, headed by Fábián Imréné.
Due to the green color of the building, it was also called the "Green School" in the public mind.
In 1988, the Budapest City Council approved the establishment of new vocational secondary schools.
The evening hours of the 3rd EPAL Alexandroupolis Vespers (18:00 – 22:30) are offered for those of you whose professional, personal or family obligations do not allow you to attend a morning school.
All these factors should not inhibit your desire to attend our school's study program and not create the conditions for changing your professional, financial and social status.
